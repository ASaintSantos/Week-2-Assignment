"The Evergreen State"

Washington: The Evergreen State. Located in the “upper left corner” of the continental United States, Washington State is the 42nd state, admitted to the union in 1889. It is bordered by the Canadian province of British Columbia to the north, Idaho to the east, Oregon to the south and the Pacific Ocean to the west.
